Erebos Gheil Regnum

About Erebos Gheil Regnum, the visual novel game. It's realm where fantasy and adventure intertwine. As a student at Fantasy University, you'll delve into a world brimming with magic, hidden secrets, unforgettable characters and events.

Themes

The themes of friendship and loyalty are central, emphasizing the importance of trust and support in overcoming challenges. The narrative intricately weaves these elements, showing how relationships evolve and strengthen over time.

Mystery and intrigue permeate the story, creating an atmosphere of suspense and curiosity. Uncover hidden truths and face enigmatic adversaries.

Courage and sacrifice, with characters frequently facing problems and making significant in-the-moment decisions. These acts highlight resilience and dedication.

Magic and power are pivotal to the story, emphasizing its fantasy elements. The use of magical abilities and ancient spells, often in combination with unique technology.

Features

The game boasts a range of exciting features designed to enhance the player's experience. These include:

Customizable Player Experience: Players can personalize their game by inputting their name and selecting text colors. These settings are saved and reflected throughout the game.

Decision-Based Outcomes: Player choices during dialogue lead to different story outcomes, adding replayability and depth.

Save and Load System: The game allows saving and loading progress, including player settings, dialogue logs, and the game's current state.

Auto Play Mode: Players can enable auto-play to progress through dialogue and scenes automatically for a hands-free experience.

Background Only Mode: This mode lets players focus on the game's visuals without UI distractions, enhancing the immersive experience.

Configurations: The UI includes interactive elements like text speed adjustment, volume controls, and fullscreen image displays. Settings can be configured through a dedicated panel.

Dialogue Log: A log keeps track of all conversations, allowing players to review previous dialogues anytime.

Gallery: The gallery lets players view character art, backgrounds, and listen to music and sound effects, with options to switch content and view images in fullscreen.

Mute/Unmute: Players can toggle the audio on and off for a customizable listening experience.
